Advent 2022

A 2022 beer advent calendar for a friend. The calendar was made from cardboard in the shape of a triangle, representing a christmas tree. Within the triangle, there were 31 "rooms". Each room contained a beer. Each room had lights and there were buttons on the bottom.

Each day my friend had to geuss which "room" was for that day by selecting a room with the buttons and pressing the ENTER button. When he would choose the correct room, the room would blink green and he could take the beer out. When it was wrong, it would blink red. If he geusses wrong three times, he had to wait 15 minutes before trying again.

Used hardware:

Espressif ESP32 microcontroller, WS2812B LED strip, 2-by-12 LCD screen, default binary buttons

Used programming languages and frameworks:

C++, C++ Standard Template Library, Arduino

Used techniques:

Makefiles
